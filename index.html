How to Use this Map

This interactive map allows you to explore recent crime data across England and Wales. You can search for specific locations, filter by crime category, and view detailed crime information. The data is from the UK Police and the map is updated when the Police release new data. The most recent updated date is displayed near the bottom of the page.

  • Click anywhere on the map within England & Wales to see recent crimes in that area.
  • Use the dropdown menu to filter crimes by category.
  • Search for a postcode to jump directly to that location.
  • Click a crime in the list to highlight it on the map.
  • Use the "Reload Crimes" button to refresh crime data for your current view.
